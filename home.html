Who We Are?

PT Mega International Sejahtera s a cold storage service provider specializing in temperature-controlled warehousing, transportation, and value-added logistics services for temperature-sensitive products.

With modern facilities and a strategic location in Cibitung, Bekasi, PT Mega International Sejahtera ensures safe and efficient cargo handling, serving industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, logistics, and agriculture.

Driven by innovation, reliability, and sustainability, PT Mega International Sejahtera is committed to shaping the future of cold chain logistics in Indonesia.
